🌨️ Winter Driving Tips

  • Drive 80-100 km/h max, increase following distance 3-4x
  • Depart early (7 AM) to beat sunset (~4 PM)
  • Gentle acceleration/braking - no sudden moves
  • Watch for moose crossings (Northern ON) & high winds (Prairies)
  • If stranded: Stay in car, run engine 10 min/hr with clear exhaust
